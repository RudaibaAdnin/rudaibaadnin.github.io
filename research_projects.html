Research Projects

My research explores how accessible, collaborative, and AI-powered technologies can support disabled people, especially blind and low-vision learners.

Accessible GenAI Tools for Blind and Low-Vision Users

This project studies how blind and low-vision people use, understand, and evaluate Generative AI tools in everyday life, learning, and accessibility-related tasks.

Game-Based AI Literacy for Blind and Low-Vision Students

This project focuses on designing accessible learning activities that help blind and low-vision students understand AI mistakes, bias, hallucinations, and responsible AI use.

This project explores how technology can support the creation of accessible STEM materials for blind and low-vision students, including tactile, text-based, and AI-assisted formats.
