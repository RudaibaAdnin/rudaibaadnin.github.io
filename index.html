ABOUT ME

I am a third-year Ph.D. student at Northeastern University Khoury College of Computer Sciences . I work in the Technology, Equity, and Accessibility (TEA) Lab . My advisor is Prof. Maitraye Das .

My research focuses on the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Computer-Supported Cooperative Work (CSCW), and Accessible Computing. I aim to develop accessible learning and collaborative technologies for disabled individuals. Currently, I am studying the accessibility of recent GenAI-powered tools from the perspective of blind and low-vision individuals.

Pronouns: She/Her

Email Address: adnin.r@northeastern.edu
